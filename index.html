How it works

Three steps. Then forget about it.

Install the extension, enter your class schedule, and ClassBot handles the rest. Every. Single. Day.

01

Install the extension

One click from the Chrome Web Store. Works on Chromebooks, Mac, Windows, and Linux. No app downloads, no setup wizards.

02

Enter your schedule

Add your meeting links, class times, and how long each period lasts. Or sync your Google Calendar and let ClassBot figure it out.

03

Let it run

ClassBot joins each class on time, mutes your mic and camera, captures the full transcript, and leaves when class is over. You review later.

Features

Everything you'd do in class. Without being there.

Auto-mute on join

Camera and microphone get muted before you even enter the meeting. No accidental broadcasts. No awkward moments.

AI responses

When the teacher calls your name, ClassBot writes a short, natural response and sends it in the chat. Sounds like you because it learns your style.

Full transcripts

Every word from every class, saved automatically. Search, review, and study from real lecture content instead of guessing what you missed.

Smart exit

ClassBot listens for dismissal phrases like "class dismissed" or "you can leave" and exits immediately. It also watches when other participants start leaving — if most of the class drops, ClassBot goes with them. Or it leaves when the scheduled time is up.
